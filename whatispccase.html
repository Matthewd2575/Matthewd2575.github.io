DESKTOP/TOWER (PC CASE)

The desktop or commonly referred to as the tower or case, is exactly what it sounds like. It is the shell that physically
holds everything together with screws or wire ties. This is where you will often screw your mobo, fans/water cooling, hdd/sdd,
and psu. PC cases come in various shapes and sizes and depending on your wants/needs they are several options on the market.
PC cases can come as just a case or sometimes can include fans already installed. PC cases also vary in size: big and monstrous
or small like the size of and Xbox/Playstation. They can come as plain as the day or can come with tempered glass so you can see
the interworkings as well as your fancy l.e.d. or rgb lighting. Click on the picture to return to the parts page. :D
