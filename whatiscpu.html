CENTRAL PROCESSING UNIT (CPU)

The central processing unit or CPU for short, is considered the most important processor within a computer.
This processor is what performs the actual computational/logic work and controlling input/output operations.
The components of a CPU are very complex and consists of integrated circuits, microprocessors, registers, buses,
cache, arithmetic logic unit and control unit. It is considered to be the "brain" of the computer.
In today's market, the two largest manufacturers of CPU processors are INTEL and AMD. I strongly recommend
researching the differences each manufacturer has to offer in their processors as they are different in their
own ways and are often geared towards one thing or another. AMD/RYZEN processors in my opinion are designed
primarily for multitasking where as INTEL processors are designed primarily for single tasks. If you plan to
game, listen to music, edit videos, graphic design, and watch youtube all at once I highly recommend going to towards
a RYZEN/AMD processor. If you just want to perform single tasks 1 maybe 2 at a time like JUST gaming or surfing the
internet or watching a movie or editing music I strongly would recommend an INTEL processor. Basically it comes down to your
