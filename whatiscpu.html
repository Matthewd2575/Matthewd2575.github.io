CENTRAL PROCESSING UNIT (CPU)

The central processing unit or CPU for short, is considered the most important processor within a computer.
This processor is what performs the actual computational/logic work and controlling input/output operations.
The components of a CPU are very complex and consists of integrated circuits, microprocessors, registers, buses,
cache, arithmetic logic unit and control unit. It is considered to be the "brain" of the computer.
