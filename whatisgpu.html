GRAPHIC PROCESSING UNIT (GPU)

The graphics processing unit (GPU) or commonly referred to as the graphics card, is what generates the graphics that
appear on your monitor or television. Sometimes a GPU is integrated within the a CPU/MOTHERBOARD but nowadays there is
what known as a "dedicated graphics cards" in which these are essentially a computer within the computer itself. These dedicated
graphics cards have the ability to provide added on graphics/details such as shading, reflextions, ray tracing, rendering, and so on.
Dedicated graphics cards vary in price and can reach several thousands of dollars. In most cases of gaming computers, it is the most
expensive part within the computer. Do like to play your games like the origin Mario Bros on NES (choppy and pixelated) or do you like
